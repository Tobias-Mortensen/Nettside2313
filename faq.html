FREQUENTLY ASKED

Information regarding Little Saint James and the Estate.

What is the "Amazing Island"?

Little Saint James is a private island in the U.S. Virgin Islands, famously known as the primary residence of Jeffrey Epstein.

What is the "Blue Striped Building"?

The building with blue horizontal stripes is often referred to as the "Temple." Its purpose remains a subject of intense public speculation and mystery.

Who has visited the island?

Flight logs and visitor records have identified various high-profile public figures, though many details remain classified or under legal review.

Can I visit?

The island was put up for sale following the owner's death and was eventually purchased for development into a luxury resort.
